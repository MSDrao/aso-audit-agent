# ASO Audit Skill

Use this skill when auditing Apple App Store listings for search visibility and conversion.

## Scoring

Score each dimension from 0 to 10 and weight it into a 100 point total:

- Title, 20 percent: primary keyword presence, 30 character limit, brand versus keyword balance, natural phrasing.
- Subtitle, 15 percent: distinct secondary keywords, benefit-driven language, 30 character limit.
- Keyword field, 15 percent: no duplicates with title/subtitle, singular forms, comma-separated without spaces, no wasted words, full 100 character use.
- Description, 10 percent: strong first three lines, benefit framing, social proof, CTA, natural keyword use.
- Screenshots, 15 percent: use up to 10 slots, first two or three show value, readable captions, cohesive design.
- App preview video, 5 percent: exists, hooks in first three seconds, 15 to 30 seconds, understandable without sound.
- Ratings and reviews, 15 percent: average rating, review volume, recent release-note/review signals, developer responsiveness if known.
- Icon, 5 percent: distinctive, simple at small sizes, category appropriate, no unreadable text.
- Conversion signals, 5 percent: promotional text, useful What's New, in-app events/custom product pages if known.
- Competitive position, 5 percent: category competitors, visual/rating gap, keyword coverage.

## Recommendation Rules

- Cite concrete evidence from the listing or fetched competitors.
- For title, subtitle, keyword field, description, or screenshot caption changes, include before and after examples.
- Prefer specific recommendations over generic advice.
- Separate quick wins from larger changes.
- Be explicit when data is unavailable from public App Store metadata.
